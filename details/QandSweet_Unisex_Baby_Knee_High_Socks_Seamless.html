Soft, Breathable & Comfortable Baby Knee High Socks

Designed for both boys and girls, these unisex baby socks combine style with practicality for everyday wear and special occasions. Made with a premium blend of 82% combed cotton, 15% polyester, and 3% spandex, they deliver unparalleled softness and breathability, keeping your little one's feet cozy and comfortable all day long.

Why Choose These Striped Knit Socks?

  • 82% Combed Cotton ensures a soft and breathable fit for sensitive baby skin.
  • 15% Polyester and 3% Spandex blend provides flexibility and durability, making them ideal for active toddlers.
